Kuza Foundation undertake policy initiatives to serve our constituent communities and promote their long-term welfare. Policy initiatives involve developing recommendations for public policy changes that can be communicated to policymakers and stakeholders. Policy initiatives may include lobbying for better funding, advocating for legislative changes, and engaging in public education and awareness campaigns. These initiatives often involve collaboration between NGOs, community leaders, and policymakers to address social, economic, and environmental issues affecting the community.
